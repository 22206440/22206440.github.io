Spots

A few favourites around Sydney. Always respect locals and the space — keep it clean.

Five Dock Skatepark

Iconic deep bowls with classic lines. Great for transition skaters.

  • Suburb: Five Dock
  • Style: Bowls / Vert
  • Good at: Sunrise, late arvo

Bondi Skatepark

By the beach with smooth concrete and a postcard backdrop.

  • Suburb: Bondi Beach
  • Style: Bowl / Street mix
  • Vibe: Busy on weekends

Waterloo Skatepark

Street-ledges, rails and a mellow bowl. Good ground and lights.

  • Suburb: Waterloo
  • Style: Street / Small bowl
  • Good for: Evening sessions
