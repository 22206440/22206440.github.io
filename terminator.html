The Terminator (1984)

In 1984 Los Angeles, a cyborg assassin — the Terminator — arrives from the future to kill Sarah Connor, whose future son will lead the human resistance against the machines. Kyle Reese, a soldier of that future, is sent back to protect her.

James Cameron’s film blends sci-fi and horror, with relentless pacing and a breakout performance by Arnold Schwarzenegger. The film’s gritty tone, practical effects, and time-travel paradoxes helped define the franchise.
