Storyline & Review

Set years after the original, a reprogrammed T-800 is sent back to protect the young John Connor from the advanced liquid-metal T-1000. The film raised the bar for action and visual effects, pairing large-scale spectacle with a surprisingly human story about fate and choice.

Schwarzenegger’s iconic turn as the protector, Linda Hamilton’s intense performance as Sarah Connor, and Robert Patrick’s chilling T-1000 helped cement T2 as one of the most influential action movies ever made.
